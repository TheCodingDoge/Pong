Welcome

Hello and welcome to @DogeyDogs first ever repository based on the game, Pong

What is Pong?

Pong is a game where you have a paddle each and you have to try and score against the other player. Essentially it is table tennis, old style.

How many players?

2.

What are the controls?

Player 1:

UP ARROW = Up DOWN ARROW = Down

Player 2:

W = Up S = Down
